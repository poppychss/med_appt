# Medical Appointment Booking

## 📌 Overview

The **Medical Appointment Booking** website is part of StayHealthy Inc.'s digital initiative to improve healthcare access in remote areas. It enables patients to connect with doctors, book appointments, and manage their health information online.

## 🎯 Objective

To provide a simple, accessible platform for patients to find doctors, schedule consultations, and receive timely medical support.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Appointment Booking** – Schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments
* **Doctor Listing** – View doctors, ratings, and search by name
* **Consultation Feedback** – Rate and review doctor interactions
* **Profile Management** – Register and manage personal & medical details
* **Easy Navigation** – User-friendly and responsive interface

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **React.js**
* **React Router DOM**
* **JavaScript (ES7+)**
* **HTML5 & CSS3**
* **reactjs-popup**
* **Figma** (UI/UX design)

## 🚀 Future Scope

* Patient medical records access
* Health tips & educational content
* Admin dashboard & analytics
